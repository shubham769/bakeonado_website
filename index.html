Our Story

Baked Fresh,
Served with Love

Bakeonado was born out of a simple belief – that great baking changes moods, marks memories, and brings people together. Since 2012, our passionate bakers have been waking up before sunrise to fill your tables with warmth.

We source locally grown ingredients, follow time-honoured recipes, and never compromise on quality. Whether it's a rustic sourdough, a tiered wedding cake, or a box of macarons – every item leaves our oven with the same care.

  • No preservatives, no shortcuts
  • Custom orders for every occasion
  • 100 % vegetarian options available
  • Eggless variants on all bestsellers
